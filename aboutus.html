About Us

Sanskrit as we know is a language that requires a lot of memorizations of tables and the basic forms of verbs in each tense and mood. However currently all this memorization material is only available in printed format or in form of unorganized content on various websites by enthusiasts. However, we aim to bring an organized approach to this process by developing a website dedicated to Sanskrit resources and education.
